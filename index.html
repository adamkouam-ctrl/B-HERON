Our Philosophy

Not a wash.
A ritual.

BHERON was founded on a single conviction: exceptional vehicles deserve exceptional care. We reject haste, shortcuts, and compromise — offering instead a meditative process that restores your vehicle to a state of perfection.

Operating across Italy and Switzerland, our mobile ateliers bring certified master detailers, professional-grade ceramic compounds, and bespoke finishing systems directly to your residence, office, or hangar.

BHERON nasce da una singola convinzione: i veicoli eccezionali meritano una cura eccezionale. Rifiutiamo la fretta, le scorciatoie e i compromessi — offrendo invece un processo meticoloso che restituisce il vostro veicolo ad uno stato di perfezione.

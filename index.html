Custom

BHERON's steam operators service more than vehicles. Our mobile steam stations are deployed for the cleaning and disinfection of outdoor and urban infrastructure — ideal for property managers, municipalities, and businesses.

We come to your location within our Italy and Switzerland service areas. All quotes for environmental services are custom — please contact us for a site visit.

  • Advertising and signage stands — steam clean of branded display units, light boxes, and outdoor panels
  • EV charging points — safe steam disinfection of public and private vehicle charging stations
  • Poles, bollards and road signs — removal of grime, graffiti residue, and weathering from metal and composite structures
  • Solar panels — gentle steam cleaning to restore energy output efficiency without abrasives
  • Outdoor furniture and terraces — hotels, restaurants, and private estates
  • Garage floors and parking structures — degreasing and steam clean of concrete and sealed surfaces

Why Steam?

◈

Only 3 litres of water per wash. Steam cleaning consumes a fraction of the water of pressure washers or hoses.

◈

No chemical runoff. Steam is the cleaning agent — no detergents or chemicals required for most applications.

◈

Disinfection included. Steam at 160°C eliminates bacteria, mould, and pathogens on contact surfaces.

◈

Safe on delicate surfaces. Electronics, coatings, vinyl graphics, and sensors are all safe under our controlled steam technique.
